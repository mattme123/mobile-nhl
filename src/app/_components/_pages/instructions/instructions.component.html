Instructions - Single Player

It's time to test your knowledge of your NHL skills! Take part in this NHL game style trivia. There are three periods in which you will be asked 10 questions each. You will have 15 seconds to answer. Each correct answer equals one point. If you answer three correct in a row, you will be awarded a "Hat Trick Bonus" and recieve an additional point. Be sure to answer your questions before time runs out. There will be a penalty kill of two questions at a reduced time if you don't. Penalties can and do stack. You must score 'shorthanded goals' in order to reset the time to its original amount. Good Luck!
